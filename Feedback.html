The Wilson Sporting Goods portfolio is made up of the iconic Wilson brand, as well as ATEC, DeMarini, EvoShield, and Louisville Slugger, and an exclusive distributor agreement with Luxilon.

Wilson Sporting Goods Co., a subsidiary of Amer Sports Corporation, is one of the world's leading manufacturers of high performance sports equipment, apparel and accessories. Since 1914, Wilson has been on the mission to help athletes everywhere reach their true potential.

Based in Chicago, USA, Wilson brings more than a century of innovation, history, and heritage across many sports including racket sports, baseball, softball, football, basketball, volleyball, soccer, and golf.

The Wilson Sporting Goods Company is an American sports equipment manufacturer based in Chicago, Illinois.

We are part of AM International, a diversified multinational group of companies trusted by millions of customers for over six decades, with a presence across Southeast Asia, South Asia, West Asia, and Europe.

For over 100 years, Wilson has been an icon on the gridiron, delivering elite performance as the Official Football of the NFL.

Wilson was named as part of Nike's roster of signature athletes in spring 2024, joining a growing team of women who have partnered with the brand to develop signature collections with deeper meaning.

A Chinese investor group led by Anta Sports Products reached a deal to acquire Finland's Amer Sports Oyj, which bought Chicago-based Wilson in 1989.
